# EcoCoin (ECC) — Tokenomics

## Core Principle
**1 ECC = 1 tonne of verified CO₂ sequestration.**

The initial supply is fixed at **100,000,000 ECC** representing 100 million tonnes of verified carbon sequestration. Additional minting is only possible once community-shared carbon data verifies sequestration beyond 100M tonnes, subject to MINTER_ROLE governance and a 1 billion token hard cap.

## Key Notes

### No Deployer Pre-mine
The contract deployer receives zero tokens at launch. Founders receive tokens exclusively through the vested **Team & Founders** wallet. This eliminates the most common investor red flag in token launches.

### Vesting (Team & Founders — 15M)
Recommended schedule: 12-month cliff, then linear monthly release over 24 months (36 months total). Prevents founders from selling immediately after launch.

### Staking & Farming Pools (13M combined)
These 13M tokens are minted directly into the contract address at deployment — no individual controls them. They are gradually paid out as staking rewards (8/10/12% APY for 7/30/90-day lock tiers) and farming rewards.

### Minting Beyond 100M
New tokens can only be minted by addresses with `MINTER_ROLE`. This role should be governed by the DAO (Community wallet) once the platform is live. Each additional mint must correspond to verified carbon sequestration data submitted by the community.

### Hard Cap
Maximum supply is capped at **1,000,000,000 ECC** (1 billion), enforced on-chain.
